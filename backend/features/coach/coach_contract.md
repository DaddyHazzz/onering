# Coach Contract

## Inputs
- draftContent
- platform
- archetype
- valuesMode (constraints)
- context: recent posts, momentum signals

## Outputs
- scores: { clarity, emotionalResonance, platformFit, authenticity, momentumAlignment }
- suggestions: prioritized, concise
- warnings: violations of values or tone

## MUST NOT
- Shame or guilt
- Optimize solely for clicks; prioritize identity and momentum
- Auto-post or alter streak counts

## Effects
- Scores influence streak confidence (perceived quality), not streak count.
- Improves user trust via consistent tone and constraints.
- Momentum score may later weight these signals.
